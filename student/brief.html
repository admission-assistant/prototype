Pre-interview Briefing

What to expect over the next fifteen minutes

Please take a moment to read the following before commencing. This is a structured assessment, not an examination of memorisation; thoughtful, considered responses are the objective.

  1. 01

    Six questions, drawn from a peer-reviewed bank.

    Each question pertains to a distinct rubric criterion. The platform may ask one adaptive follow-up per question to clarify your reasoning.

  2. 02

    Speak naturally; you are being transcribed.

    A live transcript is shown on screen. If a passage is rendered incorrectly, you may flag it for correction.

  3. 03

    Two minutes are allotted for each response.

    A countdown is presented for pacing; a wrap-up cue appears at one minute forty-five seconds. Brevity is permitted; rambling is not penalised but unnecessary either.

  4. 04

    Use the controls if you need them.

    "Repeat the question" replays the prompt. "Pause for thirty seconds" affords a brief moment to compose your thoughts.

  5. 05

    If your connection fails, return to the same link.

    The session resumes from your most recently completed question; previously recorded responses are retained.

When you are ready, proceed to the interview chamber. The first question will be presented at your initiation.

Commence the interview
