Methodology

An interview process held to the standards your committee already uses.

Each interview is conducted in three sequenced phases. The methodology is documented, auditable, and aligned with the structured-interview literature in selection science (Campion, Pulakos, et al.). Institutions retain full ownership of the rubric and the final determination.

  1. PHASE 01

    Cohort intake & configuration

    The institution submits the applicant roster as a structured dataset. The admissions office configures the rubric — criteria, weights, scoring scale — drawn from a default appropriate to the programme stream, or from the institution's own assessment framework.

  2. PHASE 02

    Structured candidate interview

    Each candidate completes a recorded interview from a verified browser session. Questions are drawn from a peer-reviewed bank, optionally extended with one adaptive follow-up per topic. Interviews are transcribed in real time and stored under the institution's data residency policy.

  3. PHASE 03

    Evaluation & faculty review

    Each transcript is scored against the institution's rubric. Every score is accompanied by a verbatim quotation from the candidate. Faculty review the dossier, override scores where warranted, and record the determination. The platform is decision-support; the institution decides.

Governance & assurance

Built for institutions that operate under regulatory scrutiny.

Higher-education admissions are governed by FERPA, GDPR, the EU AI Act, the New York Automated Employment Decision Tool law, and the institution's own academic policies. Admissions Assistant is engineered to satisfy each — and to give your office the audit artefacts to demonstrate it.

Data residency
Hosting in the United States, European Union, or India per institutional preference. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
Evidentiary scoring
Every score traces to a verbatim quotation from the candidate transcript. Auditable end to end.
Bias audit per cycle
A formal disparate-impact analysis is delivered with the cycle results, prepared in line with selection-science guidance.
Human determination
The platform is decision-support. Faculty review, override, and record the final admissions decision under the institution's policy.
Standards & certifications
SOC 2 Type II (in progress), ISO 27001 alignment, FERPA & GDPR data-processing terms, EU AI Act compliance posture.
Operational reliability
99.9% availability during admissions windows; concurrent capacity of 5,000 sessions; resumable interviews on connection loss.
